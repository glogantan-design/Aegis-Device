Find Your Device ID

Follow these steps to locate your ring's unique hardware identifier.

1

Press the ring button 5 times quickly.

2

Wait for 3 vibrations (Haptic Confirmation).

3

Open your phone's Wi-Fi Settings.

4

Connect to "Aegis Ring [ID]".

The unique code in the Wi-Fi name is your Device ID.
