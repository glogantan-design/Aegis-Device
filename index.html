The Technology

Dual-Core Processor

ESP32 backend manages real-time button logic and low-power sleep cycles.

Independent Cellular

Integrated SIM7080G modem sends SMS alerts even if your phone is missing or dead.

Precision GPS

Attaches a Google Maps link to every emergency alert with 2.5m accuracy.

Operational Guide

Double-Click: Haptic Status Check
Triple-Tap: Trigger Emergency SOS
Hold 5s: Deep Sleep (Power Down)

Haptic Language

1 Pulse: System OK
2 Short: GPS Search
3 Short: Low Battery
5 Fast: Critical Shutdown
